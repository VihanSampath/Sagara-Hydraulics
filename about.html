About Sagara Hydraulics

Sagara Hydraulics is a trusted hydraulic systems distributor and manufacturer in Sri Lanka. We have established ourselves as a premier provider of high-quality hydraulic products for construction, industrial, marine, and mobile machinery applications.

Our dedicated team of professionals brings expert knowledge and hands-on experience to solve even the most complex hydraulic challenges. Whether it's supplying a single seal kit or designing a complete custom hydraulic system, we approach every task with precision and commitment.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, durable, and efficient hydraulic solutions to our customers, ensuring minimal downtime and maximum productivity for their machinery.
